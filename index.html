Our Story

Tea swad is an India’s first B2B tea delivery venture, which delivers tea at offices, showrooms, shops and other work place with follows the standardization in taste and maintains high quality and hygiene in tea. We made tea with natural ingredients & mix some traditional spices to give a unique freshness in tea for our valuable customers to refresh them. Beginning with the first semi cloud kitchen set up in Surat in 2020 and started our growing journey.

What We Do

We merge tech and tea under one roof and innovate unique delivery chain for customers for easy access and improve quality of product to focusing on customers’ health. We are tie-up with corporate offices, shops, showrooms and deliver tea to them on a monthly subscription, deliver standardize taste and standard quality with maintaining hygiene.
